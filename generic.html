about me

“Believe in yourself, trust your abilities and have faith in your journey.”

I am a highly skilled data and financial analyst with 5 years of comprehensive experience across industries. My technical expertise and proven track record in executing high-quality projects make me a valuable asset in providing advisory support for litigation. I specialize in offering early data insights for strategy design and leveraging advanced solutions, defensible workflows, innovative technology, and customized approaches to gain valuable knowledge and information from data.

With proficiency in programming languages and tools such as Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI, I excel in data mining, analysis, visualization, and financial modeling. However, it is not just my technical abilities that set me apart. I am also known for my strong soft skills, including:

- Communication: I possess great communication skills, allowing me to effectively convey complex data insights to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. This skill enables me to bridge the gap between data analysis and business decision-making.

- Collaboration: I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy working with multidisciplinary teams. By actively engaging with team members, I foster open communication, encourage diverse perspectives, and contribute to a positive and productive team dynamic.

- Problem Solving: I have a strong aptitude for problem-solving, enabling me to approach complex data challenges with a logical and analytical mindset. I am adept at identifying patterns, developing innovative solutions, and making data-driven recommendations.

- Adaptability: In the fast-paced and ever-evolving field of data analysis, adaptability is crucial. I embrace change and possess the flexibility to quickly learn new tools, technologies, and methodologies. This adaptability allows me to stay at the forefront of industry trends and deliver innovative solutions to clients.

- Attention to Detail: I am meticulous in my work, paying close attention to detail to ensure the accuracy and reliability of my data analysis. I am also good at planning the quality check process for my results to make sure that my work deliverables are in good quality.

Prior to joining Kroll, I served as a senior associate at PwC Taiwan in the audit assurance practice. The technical skills and experience help me discover opportunities, provide insights to stakeholders for making informed business decisions, and drive business efficiencies and effectiveness.
